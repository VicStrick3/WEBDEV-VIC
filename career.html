Career Vision

Building a Financial Career Centered Around Integrity, Leadership, and Impact

Career Aspirations

I aspire to become a financial analyst in a leading firm where I can apply my skills in valuation, investments, and reporting. My ultimate goal is to work in corporate finance or private equity, contributing to strategic financial decisions that shape organizations and benefit communities.

Short-Term Goals

  • Complete a 2025 summer internship in finance or accounting
  • Get accepted into the BYU Marriott School of Business
  • Earn additional certifications (e.g., Bloomberg Market Concepts, Excel Specialist)
  • Join finance-related student clubs and participate in case competitions

Long-Term Goals

  1. Work as a financial analyst at a Fortune 500 company
  2. Obtain an MBA or CFA certification
  3. Become a CFO or investment portfolio manager
  4. Mentor underrepresented students in business fields
